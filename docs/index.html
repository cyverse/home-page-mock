"Transforming Science through Data-driven Discovery"

CyVerse fills a niche created by the computing era and ability to more rapidly amass data than analyze it. Our powerful extensible platforms provide life scientists with computational infrastructure to handle massive datasets and complex analyses, thus enabling data-driven discovery and collaborations.

Access

Bring your dataset or computational problem, study publicly accessible data hosted in our data commons, or federate with our resources to streamline your project. All available at the touch of a keypad from anywhere in the world with an Internet connection.

Learn More
Analyze

Manage your data or project with our suite of platforms for secure storage and analysis, or integrate your own software tools. CyVerse provides high-throughput computing allocations and cloud-based virtual machines so you can customize your computation environment.

Learn More
Share

Give colleagues around the world specialized access to your dataset or project, or make your completed dataset publicly accessible, track-able with a unique digital object identifier. Empower other researchers or students with our educational curricula.
